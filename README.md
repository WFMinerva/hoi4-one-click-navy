# One-Click Navy Builder

[![Steam Workshop](https://img.shields.io/badge/Steam-Workshop-1b2838?logo=steam&logoColor=white)](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3765832437)

A sandbox-oriented naval expansion mod for **Hearts of Iron IV**.

This mod adds player-only decisions that allow supported countries to rapidly establish historical or alternative naval forces without manually designing every ship or managing long construction queues.

Designed for single-player sandbox gameplay.

---

## Features

### Player-Only Naval Decisions

The mod provides special decisions available only to the player.

The AI will not use these decisions.

The purpose is to provide a faster and more convenient sandbox experience while keeping normal game progression unchanged.

---

## Supported Countries

Current supported countries:

- Republic of China (CHI)
- Communist China (PRC)
- Manchukuo (MAN)

More countries may be added in future updates.

---

# Republic of China / 中华民国

## Modernized Main Fleet

A rebuilt modern naval force representing an alternative development path for the Republic of China.

Includes:

- Aircraft carriers
- Battleships
- Light cruisers
- Destroyers
- Carrier-based naval aircraft
- Required naval technologies

---

## Rebuild the Beiyang Fleet

A commemorative reconstruction of the historical Beiyang Fleet.

This option provides a dedicated fleet inspired by China's first modern navy and its historical legacy.

---

# Communist China / 中国共产党

## People's Navy Development

Creates an early People's Liberation Army Navy force.

The design focuses on:

- Early naval capability
- Soviet-influenced development
- Historical atmosphere

---

# Manchukuo / 满洲国

Provides a preset naval force based on the Japanese naval influence within the Manchukuo scenario.

---

## Design Principles

This mod follows several principles:

- Player-only functionality
- No AI usage
- No changes to national focus trees
- No changes to historical events
- No interference with unrelated countries
- Designed for single-player sandbox gameplay

The mod does not aim to provide historical balance. It provides a powerful alternative sandbox experience.

---

## Compatibility

Designed for:

- Hearts of Iron IV 1.19.x
- Vanilla-focused gameplay

The mod only adds decisions, technologies, ship designs, and related content required for its own features.
